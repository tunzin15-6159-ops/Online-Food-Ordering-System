About FoodNest

📌 Project Overview

FoodNest is a web-based online food ordering system developed as our lab final project. It allows users to explore food categories, select restaurants, add items to cart, manage quantity, and place orders using online or offline payment methods.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple, fast, and user-friendly food ordering platform that connects customers with their favorite restaurants.

🌍 Our Vision

We aim to build a reliable and efficient system that enhances the online food ordering experience using modern web technologies.

✨ Key Features

  • Multiple Food Categories
  • Restaurant Selection
  • Full Cart System
  • Quantity Control
  • Online & Offline Payment
  • Rating & Feedback System

💻 Technologies Used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • LocalStorage
